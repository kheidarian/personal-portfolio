Kevin Heidarian

Artistic designer and developer


Hello. My name is Kevin Heidarian. I am an avid web designer and front end developer. I am constantly learning new skills that enable me to find more creative and efficient ways to create various responsive web pages and to make them more user interactive.

I have been learning web technology for the past two years. I always strive to find better ways to create responsive web pages that are compatible with all web browsers and devices. I am always working to improve my skills to make better web pages for myself and for my clients.

I am also an artist. I have been drawing for most of my life. I approach web design and development with the same mindset as making a work of art. I like to plan it out and I always find new ways to create stunning works of art.

With my artistic talent and intellect I strive to make web pages more aesthetic and functional. I believe that having a good eye for design is important to create a simple yet engaging website.
